The Home page serves as a quick overview of the company's information.
This page is completely customizable through Widgets.
Widgets are components that can be added, moved, and deleted from the home page. Widgets can be of different types, such as charts, lists, etc.

This page is independent for each company you have access to and the widgets vary according to the user's permissions.
