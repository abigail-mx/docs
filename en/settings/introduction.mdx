# Introduction: Managing Preferences and Personal Information

To access the settings, you need access to the **Settings** module.

## Navigation and Access

1. **Access the Settings Module:**
   - In the navigation bar, find the **Settings** section and click on it.

## Module Information

This module is always available and allows you to manage your preferences and personal information.

### Module Sections

1. **Preferences:**
   - Change the application language and choose between light mode and dark mode.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/settings-preferences-en.png"
     alt="Settings Preferences"
   />

2. **Personal Information:**
   - Edit your basic information and add a profile picture. This information is account-wide and shared across all companies where you are active.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/settings-information-en.png"
     alt="Personal Information"
   />

3. **Change Password:**
   - Use the form to change your password.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/settings-password-en.png"
     alt="Change Password"
   />

4. **Active Companies:**
   - View a list of the companies where you are active along with the role you hold in each. You can remove your account from a specific company by clicking the trash can icon.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/settings-companies-en.png"
     alt="Active Companies"
   />
