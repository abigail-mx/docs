# Suppliers: Efficient Management of Suppliers and Contacts

To view suppliers, you need access to the **Suppliers** module.

## Navigation and Access

1. **Access the Suppliers Module:**
   - In the navigation bar, find and select the **Suppliers** section.
   - Click to enter the module.

## Suppliers Dashboard

Upon entering the module, you will find two sections:

1. **Suppliers List:**
   - Search for suppliers by name.
   - Entries include the supplier’s name, email, and main contact.
   - To add a new supplier, click on **Add Supplier** at the top right.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-list-en.png"
     alt="Suppliers List"
   />

   - Call, email, and WhatsApp buttons use the main contact’s information if available, otherwise the supplier’s profile data.

2. **Statistics:**
   - Blocks with relevant statistics about your suppliers.

## Supplier Profile

Click the arrow on each supplier entry to access their profile, where you can manage their information.

1. **Basic Information:**
   - Modify company information.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

2. **Products:**
   - List of products managed by the supplier.
   - Add new products or modify existing prices.
   - Abigail keeps a record of product prices over time.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

3. **Purchase History:**
   - Available if you have access to the **Purchases** module.
   - Details of purchases made from the supplier, including date and amount.
   - Quick access to purchase details.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

4. **Purchase Charts:**
   - Available if you have access to the **Purchases** module.
   - Chart of purchases by month and year, and total amount purchased from the supplier.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

5. **Contacts:**
   - Add multiple contacts for the supplier.
   - Only one main contact identified with a star.
   - Main contact information replaces supplier information in the initial list.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

6. **Locations:**
   - Manage the supplier’s locations such as warehouses or branches.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/suppliers-details-en.png"
     alt="Supplier Details"
   />

Abigail streamlines the management of suppliers and contacts, allowing you to optimize operations and improve your company’s efficiency.
