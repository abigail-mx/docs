# Accounting: Global Financial Overview

To view accounting, you need access to the **Accounting** module.

## Navigation and Access

1. **Access the Accounting Module:**
   - In the navigation bar, find and click on the **Accounting** section.

## Module Information

In this module, you can get a comprehensive view of your company’s financial status.

### Global Financial Information

1. **Total Earnings History:**
   - View the total earnings history for the current year with a chart comparing income against expenses, showing the resulting profits.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/accounting-overview-en.png"
     alt="Total Earnings History"
   />

2. **Detailed Financial Data:**
   - **Current Month Income:** Displays the income generated in the current month.
   - **Previous Month Income:** Shows the income from the previous month.
   - **Current Month Expenses:** Reflects the expenses incurred in the current month.
   - **Previous Month Expenses:** Shows the expenses from the previous month.
   - **Accounts Payable:** List of outstanding accounts with suppliers.
   - **Monthly Accounts Payable:** Breakdown of accounts payable by month.
   - **Accounts Receivable:** List of outstanding accounts with clients.
   - **Monthly Accounts Receivable:** Breakdown of accounts receivable by month.

     alt="Detailed Financial Data"
