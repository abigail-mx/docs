# My Company

To edit your company information, you need to have access to the **My Company** module.

Locate the **My Company** section in the navigation bar and click on it.

Once you have entered the **My Company** module, you can edit the company's information such as the name, email address, and logo.

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/edit-company-en.png"
  alt="Edit Company"
/>

Additionally, you can modify your company's subscription by changing the current plan, updating payment information, or canceling your subscription.
