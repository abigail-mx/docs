# Products: Management and Organization of Products

To view products, you need access to the **Products** module.

## Navigation and Access

1. **Access the Products Module:**
   - In the navigation bar, find and select the **Products** section.
   - Click to enter the module.

## Products Dashboard

This module will help you register and find your company’s products.

### Products Section

1. **Search Products:**
   - Use the search button to find products by their registered name.

2. **Add Products:**
   - Click on **Add Product**.
   - Fill in the required fields.
   - Click **Add Product** to save the product automatically.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/products-list-en.png"
     alt="Products List"
   />

### Product Categories Section

1. **Search Categories:**
   - Use the search button to find product categories.

2. **Add Categories:**
   - Click on **Add Category**.
   - Enter the category name and description (e.g., if it is consumable).

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/categories-list-en.png"
     alt="Categories List"
   />

3. **Category Details:**
   - Selecting a category will display the breakdown of products in that category.

   <img
     className="block"
     src="/images/categories-details-en.png"
     alt="Category Details"
   />

Abigail streamlines the management and organization of products, providing efficient and detailed control of your company’s products.
